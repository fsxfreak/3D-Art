# Design Document for __Rocket in the Outback__

Written by: __Leon Cheung__

You are a rocket in the Australian Outback who dearly wishes to go to the moon. 

### Game Genre: Rocket in the Outback is an Adventure Sandbox RPG in a alternate-universe setting, where rockets replace humans as the superior machine. The rocket (player) will engage in its own quest, roaming around the Australian Outback
